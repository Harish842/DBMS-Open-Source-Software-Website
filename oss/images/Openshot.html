OpenShot

About:

OpenShot is one of the leading video editors for Windows PCs. The free program is ideal for beginners, and lets you understand the
intricacies of video editing without much effort. While the ‘simple view’ is good for beginners, experienced professionals can access the
‘advanced view’ for more functionalities. With a single-click operation, OpenShot lets you import multimedia files from the PC’s hard drive.
